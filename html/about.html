Our
Story

Back in 2016 Jerry Seinfeld (no relation) and myself (Patrick LeBlanc) were talking about the impending doom our planet faces if we don't act now to save teh environment. We came to realize that in the Ottawa community there wasnt any obvious, accessible or mainstream way for people to get together, organize and act in meanigful ways to save the planet. We came to the conclusion that we needed to create a such a place, and so Café Ground was born. Since the founding of the Café we've hosted over 50 environmental speakers, 75 community clean up events and have become a vital part of the Ottawa environmental community.

Why We Care:

First off, here's what we care about: The environment, our community, our clients. As for why we care... well, because it's our obligation and our passion. At Café Ground we know that the environmental crisis is'nt going away any time soon, and as humans (who created this mess) it's our duty to act. We care about our community beacuse we understand that happiness and fullfilment come from serving and living in harmony with those around you.

Our employees are proud to serve our clients and the community

Since opening our doors in 2016 we've picked up over 5 million pieces of trash in the national capitol region
