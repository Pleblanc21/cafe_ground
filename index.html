Our Mission

Our mission at Café Ground is to serve the community fresh and delicious coffee while also acting as a starting point for local environmental activism. We see climate change as the largest issue of our time so we created our café to serve as a clear meeting point to organize around from which members can plan local clean ups, retreats, and protests all while enjoying a delicious, ethically sourced, mug of coffee.

Our Locations

We have several locations throughout the greater capitol region, including our base of opperations located near Algonquin College in Ottawa, ON.

We care about the source of our coffee

Our coffee (and all our poroducts) is ethically sourced from environmentally concious farmers. We have made it our ongoing priority to ensure that our products are sustainably sourced.
